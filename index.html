About

Uriah is not the most traditional Navajo name, but it's my middle name that I love going by. I am a young Native American developer currently living in PHX AZ. I always knew I wanted to work with tech, even at a young age. In music, I taught myself to program compositions with applications until I could be contracted for it. In pre-construction, I taught myself how to design with applications until I could be paid to do it. Years later, here I am making the applications as a career and loving it.
